CemTrack Cement Inventory Management and Delivery Tracking System

Efficient Cement Inventory Management

CemTrack helps organizations monitor cement deliveries, track supplier activity, manage silo allocations, and maintain accurate inventory records to support efficient operations and stock control.

Key Features

Delivery Tracking

Record and monitor incoming cement deliveries from suppliers such as Dangote and BUA Cement.

Inventory Monitoring

Track inventory levels and maintain accurate stock records across multiple storage silos.

Reporting

Generate reports showing delivery history, supplier activity, and inventory utilization.
